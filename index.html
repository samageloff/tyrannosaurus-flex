Flexbox

A more efficient way to lay out, align and distribute space among items in a container.

Even when their size is unknown or dynamic.

  • has been difficult to do in web layouts for years and even decades

The goal

Give the container the ability to alter the width, height and order of elements to best fit the available space

Accommodate all screen sizes and display devices

Direction agnostic
